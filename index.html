UNICEF

UNICEF works in over 190 countries and territories to save children's lives, to defend their rights, and to help them fulfill their potential, from early childhood through adolescence. If you would like to find out more about their work visit their website. UNICEF is our no1 organization, they are our visitors first choice and the one that gives back the most.
